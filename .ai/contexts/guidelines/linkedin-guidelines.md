# LinkedIn Post Style Guide

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-22
**Owner:** Mike + Sophie
**Used By:** linkedin-ideation, social-content, post drafting

---

## The Rules

### Length & Structure
- **Target:** 800-1,000 characters (~150-180 words, 16-20 sentences)
- **Reading level:** 4th grade. If it sounds smart, simplify it.
- **Max 2 sentences per paragraph.** Single-line paragraphs for punch.
- **Aggressive white space.** Empty line between every thought.
- **Mobile-first.** 72% of LinkedIn is mobile. Preview on phone.

### The Hook (Lines 1-2)
The hook is everything. Mobile truncates at ~140 characters. Desktop at ~300.

**Rules:**
- Must create tension, curiosity, or surprise
- Must earn the "see more" click — the click itself boosts reach
- Never reveal the answer in the hook
- No throat-clearing ("I've been thinking about..." / "So today I want to...")

**10 Go-To Hook Formulas:**

| # | Formula | Type |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | "I [did X]. [Unexpected result]." | Story |
| 2 | "Most [people] think [common belief]. They're wrong." | Contrarian |
| 3 | "[Specific number]% of [group] [surprising fact]." | Data |
| 4 | "Stop [common practice]. It's costing you [specific thing]." | Contrarian |
| 5 | "I lost [something significant] because of [mistake]." | Failure |
| 6 | "ONE change to [process] [dramatic result]. I tested it for [time]." | Curiosity |
| 7 | "The difference between [A] and [B]? [Unexpected answer]." | Curiosity |
| 8 | "After [X years/events], here are [N] things I know for sure." | Authority |
| 9 | "Nobody talks about [uncomfortable truth about topic]." | Contrarian |
| 10 | "[Short declarative statement]. Here's why." | Direct |

### Post Body
```
HOOK (2 lines — earns the click)

Setup (2-3 short paragraphs — the story/context)

The Insight (the actual value — framework, lesson, or take)

Key Takeaways (optional — bullets if listing, prose if storytelling)

CTA (question or invitation — end of post)

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3
```

**Body rules:**
- One idea per post. Not two. One.
- Every paragraph must earn the next paragraph
- Use the 1-3-1 rhythm: short sentence, medium, medium, short
- Lists: max 5-7 items, 1-2 lines each
- Stories: max 3-5 short paragraphs (CAR: Context → Action → Result)

### The CTA
- **Always end with a CTA.** Open question > binary choice > tag prompt
- **Best:** "What's your experience with X?" / "Agree or disagree?"
- **Good:** "DM me [word] and I'll send you [resource]"
- **Never:** "Like if you agree!" / "Share this!" (engagement bait, suppresses reach)

## Voice & Tone

### How We Sound
- **Professional but human.** Write like you'd talk to a smart peer over coffee.
- **Opinionated but respectful.** Use "I" statements: "In my experience, X doesn't work" not "Anyone who does X is wrong."
- **Specific, not vague.** Numbers, names, examples. "I reviewed 200 proposals" not "I've seen a lot of proposals."
- **Active voice.** "I built this" not "This was built by me."
- **Contractions.** "You'll" not "you will." "Don't" not "do not."

### Words That Work
- "Stop" / "Don't" (commands attention)
- Numbers ("3 ways", "47%", "after 20 years")
- "I discovered" / "I realized" / "I was wrong"
- "The truth is" / "Here's what nobody tells you"
- "In my experience" / "After testing this with 50+ clients"

### Words That Kill Credibility

**AI tells (readers assume AI wrote it):**
- "In today's [adjective] landscape..."
- "It's important to note..."
- "Let's dive in" / "Let me unpack this"
- "Here are X ways to..." (as an opener)
- "Game-changer" / "Unlock the power of"
- Overuse of m-dashes (—)

**Corporate jargon:**
- Leverage, synergy, circle back, bandwidth
- Low-hanging fruit, move the needle, at scale
- Deep dive, drill down, ecosystem

**Engagement bait (suppresses reach):**
- "Like if you agree!"
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- "Comment below!"

### Being Opinionated
- ✅ "I used to believe X. Then I discovered..."
- ✅ "In my tests, X outperformed Y by 40%"
- ✅ "This might be holding you back..."
- ❌ "Anyone who does X is wrong"
- ❌ "You MUST do this or you'll fail"
- ❌ Attacking people or companies by name

## Formatting Mechanics

### Line Breaks & Spacing
- Empty line between every paragraph
- Never more than 2 sentences without a break
- Empty line before CTA and before hashtags
- What looks spacious on desktop may be cramped on mobile

### Emojis
- **3-5 per post.** Never more than 2 per line.
- **Use for:** Bullet points, emphasis on key lines, CTA
- **Don't:** Replace words with emojis, clump together, use casual ones (🤪 💀 🥴)
- **Best:** 📈 🚀 💡 🎯 🔥 ✅ → ▸

### Hashtags
- **3-5 max.** Minimal reach impact but helps search discoverability.
- **Placement:** End of post, separated by empty line.
- **Mix:** 1 broad (#Leadership) + 2-3 niche (#ConsultingLife #PresentationDesign) + 1 branded (#SlideHeroes)
- **Format:** PascalCase (#ContentMarketing not #contentmarketing)

### Links
- **No links in post body** — reach penalty. Put in first comment.
- **Exception:** 4+ curated links (resource roundups) actually boost reach.
- **Say:** "Link in the comments" or "DM me for the link"

### Images & Media
- **Vertical images** outperform horizontal (+10% CTR). Use 1080×1350px.
- **Photos of people** get +50% engagement vs graphics.
- **Carousels** get 1.45x reach multiplier — use for frameworks, step-by-steps.
- **Polls** get 1.64x multiplier — use sparingly for genuine questions.
- **Avoid:** Stock photos, overly designed graphics, random selfies.
- **Text-only posts** are declining (-18% engagement YoY). Pair with visual when possible.

## Algorithm Awareness

- **Saves are king.** 1 save = 5x reach of 1 like. Write save-worthy content (frameworks, checklists, lists).
- **Golden hour.** First 60-90 minutes are critical. Be available to reply to every comment.
- **Reply to comments** — indirect comments (replies to comments) give 2.4x reach boost.
- **Comment on 3-5 other posts** within 30 min of posting.
- **Post lifespan:** Good posts get traction for 2-3 weeks if conversations continue.
- **Company pages are invisible.** Only 2% of feed. Always post from personal profile.

## Pre-Publish Checklist

Before posting, every post must pass:

- [ ] Hook earns the "see more" click (test: would YOU click?)
- [ ] One idea only — not two or three
- [ ] 800-1,000 characters (check length)
- [ ] Max 2 sentences per paragraph
- [ ] Empty lines between all paragraphs
- [ ] No AI tells or corporate jargon
- [ ] Reads at 4th grade level (simple, clear)
- [ ] Active voice throughout
- [ ] Specific details (numbers, names, examples) — not vague
- [ ] CTA at the end (question or invitation)
- [ ] 3-5 hashtags at the bottom
- [ ] No external links in post body
- [ ] Previewed on mobile
- [ ] Credibility lever present (why should Mike say this?)

## Content Types — When to Use What

| Type | Reach Multiplier | Best For |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| Poll | 1.64x | Genuine questions, audience research |
| Carousel | 1.45x | Frameworks, step-by-steps, teardowns |
| Image + text | 1.18x | Personal stories, behind-the-scenes |
| Video (vertical) | 1.10x | Authenticity, personality, demos |
| Text-only | 0.88x | Hot takes, short insights (declining) |

**Default:** Pair text with an image or carousel. Pure text-only is the weakest format.
