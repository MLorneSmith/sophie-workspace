## Primary Personas

### 1. Solo Consultant
**File:** `solo-consultant.md`

**Profile:** Independent consultants creating client deliverables.

**Key Pain Point:** "I spend 15-20 hours per pitch deck."

**Value Prop:** "Deliverables that compete with Big 4 quality — at solo prices."

**Decision:** Self-funded, immediate ROI focus.

---

### 2. Boutique Consultancy
**File:** `boutique-consultancy.md`

**Profile:** Small consulting firms (2-20 people) needing consistent team quality.

**Key Pain Point:** "Our deliverables are inconsistent — partner decks vs. associate decks."

**Value Prop:** "Consistent, executive-ready deliverables across your entire team."

**Decision:** Partner approval, team license pricing.

---

### 3. Corporate Professional
**File:** `corporate-professional.md`

**Profile:** Mid-level corporate employees presenting to executives.

**Key Pain Point:** "I do great work but don't get recognized."

**Value Prop:** "The presentation skills that get you promoted."

**Decision:** Often self-funded, sometimes L&D budget.

## Persona Selection Guide

When creating content, ask:

1. **Who is this for?** Pick one primary persona.
2. **What's their key pain point?** Lead with it.
3. **What's the value prop?** Match to persona.
4. **What proof matters to them?** Consultants want methodology; corporate wants career impact.
