## Notes on Paid vs Organic Focus

All experts listed focus primarily on **organic LinkedIn outbound** with minimal emphasis on paid LinkedIn ads. The methodology is:

1. Build credibility through content
2. Engage with target buyers
3. Warm relationships before outreach
4. Use Sales Navigator for targeting (organic tool)
5. Personalized, value-first messaging

For a consultancy targeting consultancies, organic is the right approach — consultants buy from experts they trust, not from ads.
