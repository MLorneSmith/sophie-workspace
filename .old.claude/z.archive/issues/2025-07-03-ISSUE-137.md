The account settings E2E tests are failing because they are unable to authenticate and navigate to the settings page. The test attempts to sign up a new user and complete onboarding to reach `/home/settings`, but gets stuck on the sign-in page. This appears to be related to the onboarding flow not completing properly for new users in the test environment.
