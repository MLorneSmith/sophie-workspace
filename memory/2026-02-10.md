## ICP Scoring â€” Three-Circle Venn Framework (#422)

Mike shared a GTM podcast (https://youtu.be/zuCh9jTgz8g) featuring Sohom (RevOps expert). Key framework:

**Three separate scores, look at the overlap:**
1. **ICP/Firmographic Score** â€” Static fit: industry, size, technographics
2. **Engagement Score** â€” Interaction with YOUR content: website, events, emails, content downloads
3. **Readiness Score** â€” Narrowest but most powerful: pricing page visits, compliance page checks, job posting velocity, website changes indicating active need

**Key insight:** No single circle matters alone. The intersection of all three = "talk to these accounts NOW." Score them SEPARATELY, don't mash into one composite number.

**6 hypotheses proposed for SlideHeroes:**
1. "Consulting DNA" firmographic fit (consulting/advisory = highest)
2. "Sweet Spot" company size (5-200 employees)
3. "PowerPoint Pain" technographic signal (PPT users without presentation tools)
4. "Content Velocity" behavioral trigger (presentation created <48h, reference upload)
5. "Decision-Maker" role fit (Partner/Principal/Director = buyers)
6. "Trigger Event" intent signals (funding, hiring, job posts mentioning presentations)

Hypotheses 1-3,5 map to Circle 1 (ICP), 4 maps to Circle 2 (Engagement), 6 maps to Circle 3 (Readiness).
