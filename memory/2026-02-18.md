### Agentic Layer — 6 Open Decisions Resolved

3. **Data sources:** Two-layer — Netrows (person enrichment) + web search/LLM (company research). Proxycurl shut down.

- Agents available from Outline onward as persistent side panel
- Templates selected at Generate step
