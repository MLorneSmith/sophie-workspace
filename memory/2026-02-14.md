## [15:50] ICP Scoring Pipeline Built in dbt (Task #429 Done)

Built `core.icp_scores` model scoring 3,650 companies on 8 dimensions (0-100 scale):
- **Firmographic (0-40):** firm_size, industry, revenue, geography
- **Behavioral (0-30):** engagement (PostHog events), payment (Stripe subs)
- **Growth (0-15):** 12m headcount growth from Apollo
- **Recency (0-15):** account creation date

**Key design decisions:**
- **Revenue as firm-size proxy** — Apollo API doesn't return employee headcount (only growth rates), Attio `employee_range` is all null. Used revenue brackets (~$200K/employee for consultancies) to infer size.
- **Apollo `languages` field for geography** — Attio `primary_location` is mostly null. Languages[0] = "English" → score 8, English anywhere → 6. Improved from 96% unknown to meaningful distribution.
- **Segment classification includes Professional Services** — not just "Management Consulting" SIC codes. Both qualify for Primary/Secondary segments.

**Results:** 3 Primary, 29 Mid-size, 39 Solo, 173 Other Consulting. Most companies score C/F because behavioral signals (engagement + payment) are all zero pre-launch — 30 points of scoring capacity waiting to activate.

**New dbt models:** `stg_apollo_organizations`, `stg_posthog_persons`, `icp_scores`. Enhanced `dim_companies` with Apollo data (full outer join on domain).

**Pipeline runs nightly:** 6:00-6:55 AM ET (syncs) → 7:00 AM ET (dbt transform including ICP scoring).
