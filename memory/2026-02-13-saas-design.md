## Task
Analyze onboarding screenshots from Webflow, Gamma, Beautiful.ai for design patterns applicable to SlideHeroes.

## Key Findings

### Webflow = Gold Standard
Post-onboarding activation via in-product checklist + micro-tutorial overlays is the pattern to adopt.

### Consultant Personalization
Both Gamma and Beautiful.ai include "Consultant" as a role option — SlideHeroes should make consultant personalization the differentiator from step 1.

### Beautiful.ai "Select 3" Pattern
"Select up to 3 deliverable types" pattern is perfect for consultants to indicate they make strategy decks, client proposals, board presentations, etc.
