- Promise a concrete outcome
- Quick credibility (why trust us)
- Preview steps

- Reframe the problem
- Common misconception(s)

## Step 1: [Action]
- What to do
- How to do it
- Example

## Step 3: [Action]

- Pitfalls + fixes
