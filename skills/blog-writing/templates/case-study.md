# Case Study Blog Post Template

> Use for proof-driven posts. Works best when the reader can see themselves in the before-state.

---

title: "Case Study: How [persona/company] achieved [outcome]"

## Recommended Structure

# Case Study: How [X] achieved [Outcome]

Intro:
- 1–2 sentence outcome
- Who it’s for

## The Situation
- Context + constraints

## The Problem
- What wasn’t working
- Why it mattered (stakes)

## The Approach
- The key decisions
- The framework used

## The Execution (What we did)
- Step-by-step
- Artifacts created

## The Result
- Metrics (if available)
- Qualitative wins

## What We’d Do Differently
- Honest reflection

## Key Takeaways (for the reader)
- 3–7 bullets

## Conclusion + CTA
- Invite next step (soft)
