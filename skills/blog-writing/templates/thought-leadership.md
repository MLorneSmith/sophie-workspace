# Thought Leadership Template

> Use for POV/opinion pieces and contrarian takes. Goal: clarity + authority, not SEO-maximization.

---

title: "[Bold claim that challenges conventional wisdom]"

## Recommended Structure

# [Bold Claim]

Intro:
- Lead with the claim
- Why it matters now
- Who should care

## The Conventional Wisdom
- What most people believe
- Why it’s attractive

## Where It Breaks
- The failure mode
- Example(s)

## A Better Model
- New framework
- Practical implications

## What This Means for You
- Actionable next steps

## Objections (optional)
- Address the 2–4 strongest pushbacks

## Conclusion
- Re-state the claim
- Soft CTA
