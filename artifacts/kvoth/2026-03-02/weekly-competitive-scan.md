## Market Observations

### What's Working in the Market
1. **AI Agents** — The agentic AI trend is accelerating. Gamma and Microsoft leading.
2. **Data Integration** — Live Snapshots (Beautiful.ai, Plus AI) shows demand for auto-updating presentations
3. **API Availability** — Gamma's API opens enterprise automation

### What's Not Working
1. **Consulting-specific features** — Still no tool offering Pyramid Principle, SCQA, or MECE-native AI
2. **Structured thinking** — All tools generate content; none enforce logical argumentation frameworks

## Recommendations for SlideHeroes

### Priority Actions

1. **Monitor Microsoft Copilot Agent Mode**
   - Test when released (mid-March 2026)
   - Evaluate: does enterprise context help or hurt consultant workflows?
   - Risk: If Microsoft solves "blank page" problem, consultants may not need specialized tools

2. **Consider Plus AI as a Benchmark**
   - Plus AI shows Google Slides can be competitive
   - What features make them "best balance"?
   - Could inform SlideHeroes' Google Slides add-on strategy

3. **Double Down on Structure**
   - Every competitor is improving AI generation
   - The gap remains: **no tool enforces Pyramid Principle or SCQA**
   - This is SlideHeroes' defensible moat

4. **Positioning Test**
   - Consider messaging around "AI that thinks like a consultant"
   - Competitors market speed; SlideHeroes can market **quality of thinking**

### Feature Ideas to Explore

- **Framework-aware AI:** AI that suggests issue trees, pyramid structures, hypothesis-driven framing
- **Consulting template library:** MECE categorization, Porter's Five Forces, SWOT frameworks
- **Export to consulting formats:** Executive summary templates, client-ready layouts
- **Integration:** PowerPoint export with preserved structure (competitors struggle here)
